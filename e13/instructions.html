Instructions

  1. Open AdobeXD and create a artboard 900 x 1080
  2. Find the center and create a x and y axis, horizontal and vertical line, from the center (vertex)
  3. Make a 100 x 100 square (square1), rotate it 45 degrees, align the right corner with the vertex to the left of the y-axis
  4. Make a 115 x 115 square (square2), rotate it 45 degrees, place and align it with square1 to the left vertically
  5. Make a 43 x 43 square (square3), rotate it 45 degrees, place and align it with square2 to the left
  6. Highlight squares 1, 2, 3, copy and paste, flip the new squares vertically, and place them opposite on the right side
  7. Make sure the horizontal squares are perfectly aligned
  8. Make a 200 x 200 square (square4), rotate it 45 degrees, align the bottom corner with the very center, sides of square 1 and 4 should align
  9. Make a 113 x 113 square (square5), rotate it 45 degrees, make it clear, and align its center with the top corner of square4
  10. Highlight squares 4 and 5, copy and paste, flip the new squares horizontally, and place them below the x-axis
  11. Make sure the vertical squares are perfectly aligned
  12. Make a 513 x 513 square, rotate it 45 degrees, make it clear, and align the corners with the most outer corners of squares 3 and 5
  13. Make a 50 x 50 square and align its center with the vertex
